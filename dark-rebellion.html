Description

Unleash your inner rebel. Step into a world of mystery and seduction with this captivating fragrance.

Opening notes of pineapple, star anise and cardamom provide a fruity and spicy allure. As the fragrance evolves, lavender, sea notes and violet bring a refreshing and calming sensation, while base notes of teakwood, amber, vetiver and vanilla add depth and warmth — enveloping you in an aura of intrigue.

From the zesty start to the lingering intrigue, Dark Rebellion exudes charisma and leaves an unforgettable mark.

Masculine, ambery and bold — inspired by Carolina Herrera 212 VIP Black.
